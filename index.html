About Paddle

Paddle says this about himself: I'm a Scratch Team member located in Maine. I love combining my interest in Scratch with woodworking and making Scratch projects that interact with the real world in some way. Learn more about him by watching the Scratch project below!

Paddle Inside Scratch

Inside Scratch Paddle is a Scratch Team member who has over 5500 followers! He helps make sure the forums are nice and clean, and helps plan the future of Scratch, as he is an administrator. He also helps curate ST studios for events like Scratch Day, or April Fools every now and then.
